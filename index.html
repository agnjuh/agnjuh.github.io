About

I work at the intersection of molecular biology, regenerative and developmental biology, spatial and imaging-based technologies, and computational genomics. My work brings together stem cell systems, organoids, microscopy, and genome engineering with data-driven analysis of large-scale omics datasets.

I develop reproducible pipelines for RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, ChIP-seq, spatial transcriptomics, GWAS/G×E modelling, and machine learning workflows in Python and R. My scientific interests include genetic and epigenetic regulation, tissue regeneration, signalling dynamics, and genome maintenance.

Research focus

  • Genetic and epigenetic regulation of development, regeneration, and tissue homeostasis
  • Spatial and imaging-based analysis of cellular architecture and gene expression
  • Experimental systems: CRISPR genome editing, stem cells, organoids, advanced microscopy
  • Computational genomics: RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, ChIP-seq, variant analysis, integrative multi-omics
  • Machine learning for feature extraction, classification, and biological prediction
  • Reproducible workflow design and automation for high-dimensional datasets
